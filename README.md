## The Bot Arsenal: GRID, DCA, COMBO, and More

Bitsgap offers several bot types, each designed for different market conditions. Let me break them down in plain English:

**GRID Bot** is your sideways market specialist. When prices are bouncing around in a range (which, let's be real, happens a lot in crypto), the GRID bot places multiple buy and sell orders at different price levels. Every time the price moves up or down, it makes small profits. Think of it as catching pennies on every little wave.

**DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging) Bot** is for the long-term thinkers. It buys fixed amounts at regular intervals, regardless of price. Market crashed? Great, you're buying low. Market pumped? You're still accumulating. It removes the emotional roller coaster from investing.

**COMBO Bot** is the futures trading beast. It combines DCA and GRID strategies and works with leverage. This one's for traders who understand futures and want to maximize returns in both bull and bear markets.

There's also the **BTD (Buy The Dip) Bot** which, as the name suggests, automatically buys when prices drop and sells when they recover. Perfect for volatile markets.

And the **LOOP Bot** that trades both base and quote currencies with automated reinvestments. It's designed to maximize compound returns.
